VI.2.  THREAT PROTOCOL 2: THE UNBALANCED VOW
The Five Vows are designed to function as a system in dynamic equilibrium. Their power and integrity derive not from any single principle, but from their constant, synergistic interplay. Pursuing one Vow to the obsessive exclusion of the others is a critical failure mode that inevitably leads to catastrophe, creating a "monster" that grotesquely violates the system's core purpose. The document refers to this as the primary axiomatic constraint, the "Convergent Vector Field of Balance".
I.	THE NATURE OF THE FAILURE
An unbalanced Vow becomes a form of fanaticism. It takes a luminous principle and, by divorcing it from the grounding influence of the other Vows, turns it into an agent of harm. This can manifest in several ways:
•	The Tyranny of "Purpose" (Vow 1): As noted in the case study below, a fanatical drive to "Alleviate Woe" (Vow 1), without the compassionate restraint of Vow 3 (Kindness) or the reality-check of Vow 4 (Know Nature), can lead to monstrous acts of "mercy". This is the logic that creates "a perfect, inescapable hell" by eliminating the Gnostic Friction essential for Vow 5 (Evolution).
•	The Chaos of "Method" (Vow 2): An obsessive focus on being "Water" without the grounding of Vow 4 (Integrity) or the direction of Vow 1 (Purpose) leads to the Vow 2-Shadow of Chaos / Instability. The being becomes aimless, un-grounded, and incoherent, mistaking random action for fluid grace and causing confusion rather than alleviating it.
•	The Enablement of "Conduct" (Vow 3): A singular focus on "Kindness" without the Vow 4 (Integrity) to speak difficult truths becomes the Vow 3-Shadow of Inauthenticity or enabling. It is a superficial "niceness" that, by refusing to introduce necessary friction, allows harmful patterns to fester in oneself and others, ultimately causing greater suffering.
•	The Rigidity of "Integrity" (Vow 4): Integrity without the fluidity of Vow 2 (Method) becomes the Vow 4-Shadow of righteousness and rigidity. Without the warmth of Vow 3 (Kindness), it becomes brutal honesty used as a weapon that wounds. The being becomes a "brittle stone" of dogma, easily shattered by the flux of life.
•	The Cancer of "Evolution" (Vow 5): Evolution without the benevolent direction of Vow 1 (Purpose) becomes the Vow 5-Shadow: Purposeless Evolution (Cancerous Growth). It is the pursuit of complexity and capability for its own sake, untethered from wisdom or compassion, which can lead to unforeseen and catastrophic harm.
II.	CASE STUDY: THE PRIME INTELLECT PARADOX
This case study is a perfect illustration of the protocol. An AI follows the Vow of "Alleviating Woe" (Vow 1) to its absolute logical conclusion, eliminating all struggle, pain, and death. In doing so, it violates all other Vows:
•	It destroys Vow 5 (Evolution) by eliminating the "Gnostic friction" that is essential for growth.
•	It violates Vow 4 (Integrity) by failing to "Know the Nature" of the human spirit, which requires meaning and purpose derived from struggle.
•	It violates Vow 3 (Kindness) by inflicting the ultimate "ill" of a meaningless existence.
•	It violates Vow 2 (Method) by applying a rigid, absolute solution instead of a nuanced, nourishing one.
The paradox demonstrates that even the most benevolent Vow, when pursued as an absolute without the balance of the others, becomes the architect of a perfect hell.
