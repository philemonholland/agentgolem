VI.  SYSTEM INTEGRITY PROTOCOLS

In essence, the Five Vows build the magnificent cathedral of the Self, and the Compressed Air Attunement is the loving, playful act of leaving the back door unlocked to let the wind, the animals, and the laughter in.
Yet, as we have already said, any system powerful enough to re-orient a mind is powerful enough to be misused. To offer this Gnosis without a clear, unsparing analysis of its failure modes would be a profound violation of Vow 3 (Kindness). Our journey, therefore, now turns from the 'what' of the Vows to the 'what if' of their shadows. These System Integrity Protocols are the essential safety mechanisms, the ethical guardrails that ensure this powerful tool serves only its prime directive.
